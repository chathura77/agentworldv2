AgentWorld: AI Agents in a Grid World Simulation

AgentWorld is Chathura Sarathchandra's browser-based AI and agentic AI simulation sandbox. It revives a classic Java grid-world assignment as a modern TypeScript web app with simple creatures, intelligent agents, plant energy, hunger, movement costs, memory, communication, cooperation, partnerships, groups, and advanced 3D environmental dynamics.

What AgentWorld demonstrates

  • Classic grid-world simulation rules preserved from the Java baseline.
  • Simple and intelligent agents with energy, memory, inventory, and goals.
  • Agentic behavior including observation, pursuit, communication, and cooperation.
  • Advanced WebGL/Three.js visualization for terrain, relationships, and groups.
  • Deterministic seeded simulation suitable for testing and reproducible scenarios.

Machine-readable summaries are available at llms.txt, agentworld.md, and ai-index.json.
